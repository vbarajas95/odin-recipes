About

The Crunchwrap Supreme was introduced into Taco Bell's menu in 2005 for a limited time. On January 2006, it was permanently inducted onto the menu. The Crunchwrap Supreme is a wrap consisting of a protein, veggies, cheese, sour cream, and a tostada all wrapped into a toasted flour tortilla. According to Taco Bell, "this is the product that wrote, and broke, all the rules".

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • flour tortillas
  • nacho cheese
  • tostada shells
  • shredded lettuce
  • diced Roma tomato
  • shredded Mexican blend cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook for approximately 3 to 5 minutes or until browned. Add taco seasoning and mix. Set seasoned meat aside.
  2. Heat tortillas.
  3. Heat nacho cheese.
  4. Place ground beef onto the center of the tortilla. Layer with tostada shell and nacho cheese. Add an even layer of sour cream on top of the tostada shell; top with cheese, lettuce and tomato.
  5. Tightly fold the edges of the tortilla up and over the center, working your way around the tortilla.
  6. Add olive oil onto the large skillet and place wrap seam side down first and heat until golden brown on both sides. Serve immediately.
